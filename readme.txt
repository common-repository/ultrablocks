== Description ==

UltraBlocks is a powerful Gutenberg block plugin designed to enhance your WordPress editing experience. With a variety of custom blocks, including container, grid, paragraph, buttons, countdown, shape-divider, google-maps, and more, UltraBlocks allows you to create stunning layouts and designs effortlessly.

UltraBlocks is continually evolving, with more exciting features and blocks planned for future updates.

== Installation ==

1. **Upload & Install:** Simply drag the `ultrablocks` folder into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or get it up and running directly from the WordPress plugins screen.

2. **Activate:** Head over to the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress and hit 'Activate' to bring UltraBlocks to life.

3. **Get Started:** Dive into the fun! Start adding UltraBlocks to your Gutenberg editor and watch your content come alive.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use UltraBlocks? =

Once the plugin is installed and activated, simply navigate to the Gutenberg editor in WordPress and start adding UltraBlocks from the block inserter.

= Can I customize the blocks? =

Yes, each UltraBlock comes with various customization options, allowing you to tailor them to suit your design needs.

= Is UltraBlocks compatible with other plugins/themes? =

UltraBlocks is designed to work seamlessly with most WordPress themes and plugins. However, if you encounter any compatibility issues, feel free to reach out for support.
